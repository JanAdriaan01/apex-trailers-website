About Apex Trailers

South Africa's premier manufacturer of premium refrigerated trailers. We specialize in crafting high-quality chiller and freezer trailers that businesses trust for their cold chain transport needs.

Our Story

Apex Trailers was established with a clear mission: to provide South African businesses with the highest quality refrigerated trailers at competitive prices. Based in Midrand, Gauteng, we serve customers across the entire region and beyond.

Our team consists of experienced professionals who understand the critical importance of reliable cold chain transport. Whether you're a caterer needing to keep food fresh at an event, a distributor moving frozen goods, or a pharmaceutical company requiring precise temperature control, we have the solution for you.

We believe that every business deserves access to premium refrigerated trailers without paying premium prices. That's why we manufacture our trailers locally, using the finest materials, to deliver exceptional value to our customers.

Our Manufacturing Standards

Every Apex Trailer is built to exacting specifications using premium materials

Stainless Steel Flooring

We use premium grade 304 stainless steel for all trailer floors. This material provides exceptional durability, corrosion resistance, and easy cleaning - essential for food safety compliance and long-term use.

Aluminum Framework

Our trailers feature rigid aluminum structured framework both inside and outside. This lightweight yet strong material ensures maximum thermal efficiency while keeping the overall trailer weight manageable for towing.

IP Rated Internal Lighting

All trailers come equipped with industrial-grade IP rated internal lighting. This ensures safe operation in cold, moist environments while providing consistent, reliable illumination for loading and unloading.

Generator Mounting Points

Every trailer includes a dedicated mounting point for a portable generator. This ensures you can operate the refrigeration unit even at remote locations without access to mains power.
