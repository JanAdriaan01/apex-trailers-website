Chiller Trailer Features

Every Apex Chiller Trailer comes equipped with premium features as standard, ensuring reliable performance and ease of use.

Precise Temperature Control

Digital thermostat maintains consistent temperatures between 0°C and 4°C, ideal for fresh produce and sensitive pharmaceuticals.

Easy Cleaning

Seamless stainless steel floor and smooth aluminum walls allow for quick and thorough cleaning between loads.

Energy Efficient

High-quality insulation and efficient refrigeration system minimize power consumption while maintaining optimal temperatures.

Generator Ready

Built-in mounting point for portable generator ensures you can operate the refrigeration unit even without mains power.

Spare Wheel Fitted

Every trailer includes a spare wheel mounted securely, so you're prepared for any situation on the road.

Digital Monitoring

Easy-to-read digital thermometer displays current temperature at a glance, with alarms for temperature excursions.
