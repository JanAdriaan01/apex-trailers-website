Technical Guide

What to Expect When
Buying from Apex Trailers

Every component is engineered for durability and performance. Here's what makes our trailers built to work.

01

Tow Bar & Chassis

Heavy-duty tow bar and chassis construction designed for South African roads. Engineered to handle the weight of refrigerated cargo while maintaining stable towing characteristics.

02

Refrigerated Panels (75mm)

Our signature 75mm insulated panels provide industry-leading temperature retention. Whether you're keeping goods frozen or chilled, our panels maintain consistent temperatures efficiently.

03

Cooling Door

Specially designed cooling door with proper sealing to maintain internal temperatures. Easy to operate with secure closing mechanism. Door handle designed for frequent use.

04

Mud Guards & Wheels

Durable mudguards protect both the trailer and your cargo from road debris. Same rugged tyres fitted on every unit for reliability and easy replacement when needed.

05

Air-Conditioning Units

Reliable fridge air-conditioner unit installed on every trailer. Same cooling performance across all units. Optional generator for independent operation.

06

Generator Space

Integrated front-mounted space for generator. Neatly contained and easily accessible for maintenance. Perfect for mobile applications requiring independent power.

07

Interior Light & Switch

Interior light with dedicated switch for easy access. Illuminates the cargo area for safe loading and unloading, day or night.

08

Generator & Air-Conditioner Cages

Protective cages for both generator and air-conditioner units. Security features prevent tampering while allowing necessary ventilation.

09

Rear Bumper

Reinforced rear bumper provides protection during loading and unloading. Designed for durability and to protect the trailer structure.
